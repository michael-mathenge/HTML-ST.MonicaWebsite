High demand healthcare needs

  • Chronic desease detection e.g hypertension and diabetes through blood sugar and blood pressure monitoring.
  • Health screening, e.g. TB and HIV.
  • Health education.
  • Community based health workers training.
  • Home-based Care
  • Outreach clinics

In our examination room, we perform

  • Nebulizations for asthmatic.
  • Suction to clear airway
  • IV canulation.
  • Tepid sponging for high temperature
  • Administration of suppositories
  • Minor surgical procedures
    • Circumcision
    • Wound dressing
    • Incision and drainage of abscesses
    • Stitching of wounds
    • Stitch and staple removal
